ABOUT PIXIU

The Pixiu is a mythical creature resembling a winged lion. It is believed to attract wealth and ward off evil spirits. Pixiu are commonly used in feng shui practices to bring financial success and protection.

Today, Pixiu remains popular not only in China but also in other parts of Asia and among Feng Shui enthusiasts worldwide. It is a common gift for those starting a new business or moving into a new home, symbolizing the wish for prosperity and protection in their new endeavors.

Overall, Pixiu is a fascinating blend of mythology, cultural tradition, and practical application in modern Feng Shui, embodying the timeless human desire for wealth, security, and good fortune.
